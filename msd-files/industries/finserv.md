## Terminology

- ACH / SEPA / Faster Payments: Bank transfer rails (region-specific)
- AML (Anti-Money Laundering): Monitoring and investigation of suspicious activity
- Audit Trail / Auditability: Who accessed what, when, and why. Critical for regulators and internal risk teams
- Customers are often called "clients"
- KYC (Know Your Customer): Identity verification required to onboard and maintain accounts
- PEP (Politically exposed person): Higher risk for bribery, corruption, and financial crime
- PII (Personally Identifiable Information): Highly sensitive in fintech support, drives redaction, masking, and access controls
- FinCrime / Financial Crime Ops: Umbrella term covering fraud, AML, sanctions, scams, and investigations

## Competitive Landscape

**Niche and AI Point Solutions (HubSpot, Kustomer, Intercom, Decagon, Fresh):**
Fragmented channels lead to repeated authentication, inconsistent handling of sensitive data, and customers re-explaining high-stakes issues. Reactive AI struggles with multi-step financial scenarios. Most point solutions lack fintech-grade data protection, auditability, and AI governance.

**Legacy Enterprise Platforms (Salesforce, ServiceNow):**
Heavy customization, complex integrations, and high implementation costs delay time-to-value. Day-to-day operations depend heavily on IT and consultants. Powerful but rigid, built for global giants, not fast-moving fintechs.

## Business Challenge Overview

Fintech customer support sits on the front line of people's money, identity, and trust. Every interaction carries risk: fraud attempts, account access issues, regulatory exposure, and reputational damage, often under intense time pressure.

---

## Key Pains

**When fintechs grow fast, customers facing money-related issues want reassurance, clarity, and confidence:**
- 46% of financial services agents say customers increasingly expect personalized service (Zendesk CX Trends)
- Fraud spikes, verification backlogs, third-party outages, and growth create sudden volume surges
- Multiple platforms running side by side, agents switching between tools, slowing service and making consistency hard

**Cybersecurity is a critical concern:**
- Any breach can lead to significant financial loss, legal consequences, and reputational damage
- Unlike traditional banks, fintechs are often fully responsible for protecting customer assets with no government-backed insurance safety net

**Support teams see problems first but can't act fast enough:**
- Fraud, compliance, and risk teams rely on support signals but tooling isn't built for it
- Fragmented systems prevent insights and action
- Need immediate visibility into spikes tied to trading issues, bugs, or security-related events
