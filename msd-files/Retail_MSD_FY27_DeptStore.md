# Retail MSD FY27 - Department Store

Last updated: February 27, 2026

---

## Ideal Company Profile

- Type: B2C
- Service model: B2C, B2E, and B2B (supporting shoppers, employees, and suppliers)
- Segment: 10,000+ FTEs
- Industry: Large-format, multi-category retail. Big box, department stores.
- Service spans: pre-purchase assistance, inventory availability, curbside/in-store pickup, large-item delivery, warranties, repairs, returns, and ongoing support

---

## Buyer Personas

**Primary Buyer: Customer Service Leader**
Titles: Head/VP/Director of Customer Support/Service/Experience, Contact Center Operations

Key Pains:
- Inconsistent service quality across digital, store, delivery, and post-purchase journeys
- High contact volume driven by complex issues (delivery delays, warranty claims, repairs, inventory availability) difficult to automate end-to-end
- Disconnected systems between ecommerce, stores, warehouses, and third-party service providers leading to repeat contacts and low first-contact resolution
- Pressure to scale AI automation without breaking trust, especially for high-value purchases and exception handling
- Limited visibility into whether issues are actually resolved, not just closed

**Secondary Buyer: Head of IT**
Titles: Head/VP/Director of IT, Enterprise Applications, Digital Platforms, IT Operations

Key Pains:
- Fragmented and aging retail tech stacks spanning POS, OMS, inventory, logistics, and service platforms
- High integration burden connecting customer service to store systems, supply chain data, and external vendors
- Need for enterprise-grade AI governance, security, and auditability
- Difficulty enabling AI without creating brittle point solutions or increasing technical debt
- AI strategy and governance now cuts across all areas of the company

## One Liner

From inventory checks to delivery and repairs, Zendesk resolves Department Store service moments that drive trust, loyalty, and revenue.

## Short Product Description

Zendesk for Department Store retail is a secure, AI-powered service platform built to resolve complex, high-volume retail journeys across stores, ecommerce, delivery, and post-purchase support. By unifying channels, automation, analytics, and workforce workflows in one intuitive workspace, Zendesk connects customer data, inventory, logistics, and service teams to drive true end-to-end resolution.

---

## Product Differentiators

- **Trust - Brand-safe resolution at scale:** Built-in QA, explainability, and policy enforcement ensure AI and agents handle deliveries, warranties, and returns accurately and consistently across every store and region.
- **Precision - Resolution across complex, multi-system journeys:** Zendesk connects service workflows with order, inventory, and fulfillment data to resolve complex issues end-to-end, not just answer questions.
- **Guidance - AI that knows when to automate and when to escalate:** AI agents resolve routine requests autonomously while intelligently routing high-risk or high-revenue moments to the right human expert.
- **Unified - One platform for customers, stores, and employees:** Supports B2C, B2E, and B2B service models on one platform connecting customer service, store ops, warehouse teams, and vendors.
- **Scale - Built for peak, not just growth:** Proven performance during peak demand. Scales AI-driven resolution through seasonal spikes, promotions, and recalls.

- "Disconnected systems waste time chasing details, driving inefficiency and weakening quality at scale."
- "Heavy manual processes slow service delivery, inflate labor costs, and limit potential for scalable customer growth."

**Limited end-to-end visibility across digital, in-store, and service channels:**
- "Without comprehensive reporting, we are working in the dark and unable to hold teams accountable."

- "Without clear AI governance, organizations risk compliance issues, reputational harm, and missed opportunities."
- "A data breach or security incident can cause irreparable damage to our brand's reputation."
